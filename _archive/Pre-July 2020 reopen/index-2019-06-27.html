Directions

The patio bar is open Thursday–Sunday nights from Memorial Day until Labor Day, weather permitting.

We accept reservations on Valentine’s Day, Mother’s Day and New Year’s Eve.

For all other days, you can call up to a week in advance to be added to the waitlist.

We also serve brunch on Mother’s Day and New Year’s Day.

We are closed each year for Easter, Thanksgiving, Christmas Eve and Christmas Day.
